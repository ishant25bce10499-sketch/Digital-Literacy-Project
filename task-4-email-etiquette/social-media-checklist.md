Do's and Don'ts OF Social Media


✅ Do’s
•	Think before you post – consider how it might affect your reputation. 
•	Protect your privacy – use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings. 
•	Be respectful – engage in positive and constructive conversations. 
•	Verify information before sharing – avoid spreading misinformation. 
•	Balance your time – ensure social media doesn’t interfere with studies or sleep. 


❌ Don’ts
•	Don’t overshare personal information – like address, phone number, or sensitive details. 
•	Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech – it can harm your image and others. 
•	Don’t post inappropriate content – it may affect future academic or job opportunities. 
•	Don’t fall for scams or suspicious links – be cautious about what you click. 
•	Don’t compare yourself constantly to others – it can negatively impact mental health.
